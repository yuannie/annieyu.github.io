Welcome to Bird Island, home of the prancing Ban-ban creatures.
The creatures that inhabit Bird Island, the Ban-ban, dance to honor their tallest member.
The tallest Ban-ban springs infants up toward the Island of Adolescence where they're able to mature.
If infants miss the mark, the winds gently whisk them back to surrounding islands until their next shot at adulthood.
