Look Back was an immersive video-installation inspired by the time I met my first love in Japan.
The projection spanned three walls, surrounding viewers in an environment generated from memories from the trip.
I took a photograph from the trip and revisualized it in 3-D space, each pixel of the photo corresponding to a pillar in the environment.
Look Back invite viewers to reminisce with me on one of my favorite times in life thus far.
