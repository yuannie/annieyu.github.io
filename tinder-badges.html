IDEATION
I sketched solutions varying from AR puzzles to community comments in each place.
We decided to focus in on Badges, a feature which rewards users for simply going out and visiting the app!
INITIAL FLOW
I mocked up the flow of a user obtaining their first badge.
RESEARCH
I also looked into how Snapchat, Yelp, PokemonGO, and Swarm handled their badging systems.
VISUAL EXPLORATION
And began experimenting with badge styles.

After sketching flows and researching both UX and visual styles, I moved on to prototyping.
Version 1—badges in profile

After a user visits enough art galleries or venues, for example, they will earn a Places badge which they can view in their Profile settings!
Final Version—fullscreen takeover + badges in Places


The achievement modal could've been more grand so I made it a fullscreen takeover instead.

And navigating to Profile to see Places badges was confusing. Instead, I added a page within Places for people to see all the badges they've earned.
When people are viewing others' profiles, they can see the badges they have in common.

Mutual badges communicate the places and activities both love to engage—making people feel more real without any effort on the user's side.
